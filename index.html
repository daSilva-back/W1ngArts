Who is W1ngArt

My Name is Andreas "W1ngArt" da Silva, The name Wing has its origin in China I started to watch Chinese Martial Arts movies with Bruce Lee when I was young, Bruce Lee was practicing "Wing Chun" thats chinese Kung Fu. The "1" in W1ng is just something I found aesthetically pleasing when I watched streams of s1mple and summit1g back then. I'm a passionate Counter-Strike Gamer, Trader , Collector and Artwork Designer. I used to work with Adobe Photoshop CS2 since 2010, my passion for Counter-Strike pixels started around 2016, when my friends introduced me into the game itself. I started trading liquid items and very slowly got into niche items, stickered stuff, blue gems and rare pixels. Once you get started, you cant stop they said and its true. It started as a hobby which now is my passion. My focus is on Counter-Strike artworks for the community and its members. Creating artworks for the members of the community is refreshly satisfying as people share the same passion as me, collecting shiny and rare pixels. With my artworks I'm trying to set the Scene for something special. I did over 1000 Artworks for over 100 different people in the past years with success and i keep developing further.

Thank you for taking your time and beeing part of my passion.

Sincerely yours, W1ngArt
