What is W1ngArt.com

W1ngArt.com is a website I launched about passion and dedication for the game and the pixels in 2026. The goal here is to create a space for connection—with the art, with Counter-Strike, my journey and with yourselves. I'm trying to create aesthetic artwork to the viewers, hopefully my gallery pleases your eyes and mind with its unique grace.

Put on music, sip café, grab a snack & review the site.

Who is W1ngArt

My name is Andreas "W1ngArt" B. - da Silva, The name Wing has its origin in China I started to watch Chinese Martial Arts movies with Bruce Lee when I was young, Bruce Lee was practicing "Wing Chun" chinese Kung Fu. The "1" in W1ng is just something I found aesthetically pleasing when I watched streams of s1mple and summit1g back then. I'm a passionate Counter-Strike Player, Trader , Collector and Artwork Designer. I used to work with Adobe Photoshop CS2 since 2010, my passion for Counter-Strike pixels started around 2016, when my friends introduced me into the game itself. I started trading liquid items and very slowly got into niche items, stickered stuff, blue gems and rare pixels. Once you get started, you cant stop they said and its true. It started as a hobby which now is my passion. My focus is on Counter-Strike artwork for the community and its members. Creating artwork for the members of the community is refreshly satisfying as people share the same passion as me, collecting shiny and rare pixels. With my artworks I'm trying to set the scene for something special. I did over 1000 Artworks for over 100 different people in the past years with success and I keep developing further.

Thank you for being part of my passion and the skin community.

Sincerely yours, W1ngArt
